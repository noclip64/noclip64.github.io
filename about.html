Our Story

People often ask me if Rain's Bakery was named after the weather. The truth is, it was named after me, but the two are definitely connected. Growing up, the sound of rain on the roof was always my signal to head to the kitchen. While others saw a rainy day as a reason to stay in, I saw it as the perfect time to preheat the oven. What started as a hobby, sharing warm, moist cupcakes with neighbors to brighten up a gloomy afternoon, slowly grew into a passion. I realized that a good pastry does more than just taste sweet; it brings a little bit of sunshine to someone's day, no matter what it looks like outside. Today, every Buko Pie and Chocolate Moist tub we make still carries that same goal: to share a piece of my kitchen with you.

Rain Ray T. Garcia

Founder & Owner
